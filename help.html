Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Orders

How do I create an account?

Click the "Login" button in the top navigation, then select "Sign Up" to create your account. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also check your order status by logging into your account.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. Please contact our customer service team immediately if you need to make changes.

Products & Shopping

Are your products authentic Ghanaian goods?

Yes! All our products are sourced directly from local Ghanaian artisans. We work closely with communities across Ghana to ensure authenticity and quality.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept major credit cards, PayPal, and mobile money transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can see shipping options during checkout.

Shipping & Returns

How long does shipping take?

Domestic shipping (within Ghana): 2-5 business days
International shipping: 7-21 business days depending on destination

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for items in original condition. Please contact us to initiate a return.

Do you offer free shipping?

Free shipping is available for orders over GHS 500 within Ghana. International orders over GHS 1000 also qualify for free shipping.
